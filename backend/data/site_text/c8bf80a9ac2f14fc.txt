Lower costs on insurance

There are a few things you can do to bring costs down on your car, home, landlord, health and/or life insurance including:

Consider adjusting your excess to reduce your premium , though you’ll pay a higher excess if you need to claim later

Review the optional covers on your policy and choose which ones to include or remove, based on your needs

Update the agreed value of your car

Review your building and contents sum insured with the 
Building Calculator
 and 
Contents Calculator

You can also choose to pay your car, home and landlord insurance monthly with no additional premium

Check your health insurance rebate levels are still competitive.

Other resources & support

There are a range of resources that can help you with managing cost of living expenses. These include community and independent resources, as well as government organisations.

As a CommBank customer, you can access three confidential telephone counselling sessions, free of charge. The Customer Support Service provides informal and friendly discussions with a qualified and professional counsellor if you are in need of emotional or psychological support. You can make an appointment by calling 1300 360 793.
