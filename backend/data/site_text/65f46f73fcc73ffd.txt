 Rates announcement

12 August 2025

Following the RBA cash rate decision, and after considering other relevant factors, we’ll decrease our home loan variable interest rates by 0.25% p.a. effective 22 August 2025. Existing home loan customers will be able to see their new interest rate and review their repayments from 23 August 2025 in the CommBank app and NetBank.

22 August 2025

We have also made changes to our savings products. You can log on to the CommBank app or NetBank to see the current interest rate for your Savings account. Or alternatively, you can go to our account pages below to see the most up-to-date rates.

Next steps for home loan customers

If you make Principal and Interest repayments:

With a decrease in interest rates, your minimum required repayment amount may also decrease.

From 23 August 2025
, you can check to see if your new minimum required repayment amount is lower than your current repayment amount. You can choose to keep paying your current amount or reduce it to the new minimum.

If you would like to review and adjust your repayment amount, you have the following options:

If you have a direct debit set up, you can request to reduce your direct debit amount by:

Logging in to 
NetBank
 or the 
CommBank app
 and searching 
‘Change home loan repayments’

Selecting 
‘Change my home loan repayments’

If you have multiple home loans, selecting the relevant home loan account

Under 
‘Amount’, 
selecting to pay your new minimum repayment amount and preferred start date, and following the steps to submit your request. 

If you don’t have an existing direct debit set up, you can review your minimum required repayment amount:

In the CommBank app:

Select 
‘Accounts’

Select your home loan from your accounts list

Select 
‘Manage’
 to view your minimum required repayment amount.

In NetBank:

Select your home loan from your accounts list

You can find your minimum required repayment amount in the 
‘Manage Loan’
 tab.

If you make Interest Only payments:

If you're making Interest Only payments or you have a line of credit, the interest payable and your payment will automatically decrease when the new rate takes effect on 22 August 2025.

You’ll be able to see your new interest rate in the CommBank app and NetBank from 
23 August 2025
.

In the CommBank app, you can: 

Select 
‘Accounts’

Select your home loan from your accounts list

Select 
‘Manage’
 to view your new interest rate.

In NetBank, you can: 

Select your home loan from your accounts list

You can find your new interest rate in the 
‘Overview’
 tab.

For ways to manage your home loan, visit the 
‘Manage your home loan’ page
 on our website.
