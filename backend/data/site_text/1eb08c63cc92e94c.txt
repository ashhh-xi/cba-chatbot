Tell us about your complaint

If you're not satisfied with our products, services, staff, complaint handling process, or how we handle your 
Consumer Data Right
 (CDR data), it's important we hear about it so we can make things right. We’ll take your complaint seriously, work with you to address your complaint and try to find a solution that's fair and reasonable. At all times, we follow our 
complaint handling principles
.
