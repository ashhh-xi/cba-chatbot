Our approach

As part of the Bank’s strategic priorities, we remain committed to helping Australia transition to a more prosperous, resilient and sustainable future.

We continue to embed environmental and social matters into our strategy by focusing on sustainable practices, policies and outcomes.

More information on our approach and progress is available in our 
2025 Annual Report
.

We are committed to transparent reporting.
