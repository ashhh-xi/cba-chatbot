International Money Transfers just got cheaper

When you send money overseas using NetBank or the CommBank app, we’ll waive the transfer fee. (excludes AUD to AUD).

Our international products and services

International Money Transfers

Send money quickly, easily and safely to more than 200 countries, using over 30 currencies. Your funds are secure because the transfer is made through the international banking system into your recipient’s bank account. You can send an International Money Transfer online in NetBank, with the CommBank app or in any branch.

Send money now in NetBank

International Money Transfers

Receive money from overseas straight into your CBA AUD or Foreign Currency Account. This can be done safely and easily by simply quoting our CommBank BIC/SWIFT code, 6 digit BSB, 8 digit account number and some personal details.

More about International Money Transfers

Foreign Currency Account

A tailored solution for those who regularly send money overseas or need to hold savings in another currency. You can choose from major global currencies and convert them when it suits you.
