What is Money Plan?

Managing your money effectively is important, especially in times of uncertainty and change.

Money Plan in the CommBank app gives you access to handy tools to help you better manage your money, so you can stay in control of your spending and saving – all from the convenience of an app.

Plan, budget & track your spending

Track your spending

See how much you’re spending each week, fortnight, or month. Tap the blue chart to get a clear view of the categories you’re spending the most in each period.

Set category budgets

Set weekly, fortnightly, or monthly budgets for a range of spending categories, like transport, eating out and shopping.

Discover category budgets

Manage bills & payments

Predict your upcoming bills and manage regular payments and subscriptions all in one place with Bill Sense.

Explore Bill Sense

See your cash flow

Get a clear snapshot of where your money goes each month, and see how you’re trending month-to-month.

Show me Cash Flow View

Easy ways to help you save

Track your savings

Keep a close eye on your total savings over time so you don’t stray off track, simply tap the ‘Save’ tab in Money Plan.

Set savings goals

Saving for something? Set a savings goal and track your progress against weekly targets to help you reach your goal sooner.

Discover Goal Tracker

Identify potential spare cash

With Smart Savings, you can have potential spare cash predicted and identified so you can grow your savings, pay off debt, or invest.
