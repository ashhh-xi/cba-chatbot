What is Buy a home?

Buy a home in the CommBank app gives you access to guidance, tools and insights to help you all the way through your home buying and ownership journey.

From the moment you start saving for your home loan, Buy a home can help you through each step of the home buying process. From understanding your borrowing capacity and finding a property to tracking your application and managing your home loan – all from the convenience of the app. 

Features & benefits

Access to Home Lending Specialists

Get access to personalised support at every step of your home buying and ownership journey.

Track your application in real time

The Application Status Tracker in Buy a home makes it easy to instantly resume  and track your application at any time, providing transparency through the home buying process. 

Home-in

Unlock 
fast contract reviews
 through Buy a home and be ready to bid in as little as six business hours.

Manage your loan 24/7

View your home loan account at any time and make changes to your repayments so you can reach your financial goals. Learn more about setting up or linking an offset account right in Buy a home. 

Property Insights

Search for you dream property to get estimates provided by Commbank (Listing data provided by CoreLogic)

Tool & calculators

A one-stop shop for our popular home loan calculators and tools to help you along the way, such as our borrowing calculator, upfront cost calculator and repayment calculator.

How to access Buy a home

Log on to the CommBank app

Search for ‘Buy a home’

You can also access Buy a home through the CommBank app’s dashboard screen

Tip: add Buy a home to your home screen when prompted, or add it later from your Favourites list for one-click access

If you’re new to the CommBank app, you can download it from the App Store or Google Play. Then, use your NetBank client number to register.

If you're a new CBA customer who's received conditional pre-approval or conditional eligibility, you will need to have your ID verified in person in order to register for NetBank and the CommBank app.
