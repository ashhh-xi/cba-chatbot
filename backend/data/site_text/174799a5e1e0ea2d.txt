Sustainable solutions

Sustainable finance & ESG

Our Sustainable finance and ESG team supports clients in taking actions that drive better environmental, social (ESG) outcomes, by linking their sustainability strategy to their financing arrangements.

Key capabilities include structuring and facilitating:

Use of Proceeds Instruments
 – the proceeds of capital are used to finance new or existing projects or activities display green and/or social characteristics. For example, a Green Loan, Social Bond or Sustainability Loan.

Sustainability-Linked Instruments
 – proceeds from financing are available for general corporate purposes, with the cost of funding tied to agreed targets that drive improved environmental or social outcomes. For example a Sustainability-Linked Bond, Loan or Derivative.

Explore sustainable finance

Carbon markets

Our carbon markets team enables financing and risk management solutions across global carbon markets that support our clients to implement their climate strategies.

Capabilities include:

Supporting project developers:

Finance the upfront costs of setting up carbon harvesting on your land

Manage price risk in the carbon market

Act as an intermediary so you can gain exposure to carbon credit development and acquire Australian Carbon Credit Units (ACCUs) 

Supporting businesses:

Provide upfront financing for the stream of ACCUs. The ACCUs can be sold to the market, to us, or used to offset your company’s carbon emissions profile

Facilitate your ACCU purchases

Surrender ACCUs on your behalf

Explore carbon markets

Green & sustainable trade

We are proud to be a leader in supporting underlying trade transactions that facilitate positive environmental, social or governance impacts.

Financing structures and types include:

Green trade finance
 – trade financing that supports activities that lead to improved sustainability outcomes, with solutions including:

Guarantees 

Letters of credit 

Stand-by letters of credit

Trade finance loans

Sustainability-linked trade finance
 – we provide working capital and documentary trade solutions (import/export) that offer financial incentives triggered by meeting jointly agreed sustainability performance targets. These facilities help our clients align their financial arrangements with their sustainability strategies and to take actions that drive improved environmental, social (ESG) outcomes.
