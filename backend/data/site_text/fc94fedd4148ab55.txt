Apply in app

To apply, download the CommBank app

Our app uses fraud prevention technology to keep your information safe when you sign up to CommBank. Once your profile is complete, you can apply for a product.  

Securely create your profile in under 10 minutes

You’ll need to:

Download the latest version of the CommBank app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store

Open the CommBank app and choose ‘Sign up to CommBank’ 

Select your CommBank product, provide personal details and complete an ID check using a physical ID

Continue with your product application

How to download the CommBank app:

If you’re on a computer, scan the QR code using your smartphone camera to open this page

Once you’re on this page, tap the buttons near the QR code to download the latest version of the CommBank app on the Apple App Store or Google Play store

Things you should know

Here's more information on our security capabilities
 and simple steps you can take to protect yourself every day, wherever you are online.
